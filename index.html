An expert's opinion

"Many Americans have unfortunately become a little bit too complacent with protocols," Niorn Nicholas, an advanced practice provider for Advent Health Centra Care - Urget Care, said. "It's unfortunate to see becuase masks and distancing do work, but only when people follow those guidelines and protocols."

A student's perspective

"I just wish life was back to normal," Watson Loyd, a senior at K-State, said. "I miss being around my friends and having normal classes. As much as I dislike getting up early, I love being around people in class. My education hasn't been as good, and I wish people would just take this thing as more of a threat, because it is."

Somebody who has had COVID-19

"I know it impacts people differently, but for me, it was no big deal," Joe Drilling, someobdy who has tested positive for COVID-19, said. "I lost my taste and smell, which sucked. Other than that though, I was just a little tired and everything else was fine for me. I was lucky and recovered early on in my quarantine."
